OUR STORY

*

From the time of Ancient Goryeo, there stood a mystic mountain called Yeppun San. In the dark hour of winter and famine, the mountain would provide food for any who sought it. Villages were saved, and civilization continued on.

We hope to continue the longstanding tradition of community to provide you meals that are simple, fresh, and wholesome. Nourishment that not only satisfies the belly -- but fuels the soul and recharges the mind.
